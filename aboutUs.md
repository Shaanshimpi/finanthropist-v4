# About Us

## Welcome to Finanthropist

Finanthropist Educare Pvt Ltd is Maharashtra's leading stock market education institute, dedicated to empowering individuals and families with comprehensive financial knowledge. Since 2017, we have successfully trained over 10,000 families, helping them achieve financial independence through practical trading education.

Our mission is to make stock market education accessible to everyone, regardless of their educational background. We believe in teaching complex financial concepts in simple, easy-to-understand Marathi-English language, ensuring that every learner can grasp the fundamentals of trading and investing.
